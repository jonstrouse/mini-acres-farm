Blueberry Pickin'

Our blueberries are fun to pick and delicious to eat. Just think, you could be making blueberry pancakes this weekend, if only you just had some. Sure, you could get some from the grocery store, but you have no idea where those things have been!! You know exactly where our blueberries come from because you pluck those ripe little fellas yourself!

Season

Picking normally begins in mid-June through the end of July.
Hours: Tuesday 4-7pm, Saturdays 8-12pm.

Cost

$8 per half gallon

Please check our Facebook page for updates during the season before coming to the farm since we could close or delay opening due to crop availability or the weather.
