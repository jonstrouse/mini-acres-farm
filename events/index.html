Our Story

After hearing several of our blueberry customers say that our farm would be the perfect location for a party or wedding, we decided to go for it! If your dream event includes a wooden outdoor pavilion, adjacent field, a stable with access to our miniature donkeys and pygmy goats, a pond with a fountain all located on a blueberry farm, we would love to turn your dream into reality. Give us a call or send us a message today to schedule a visit or book an event.
